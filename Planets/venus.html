VENUS

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is Earth’s closest planetary neighbor. It’s similar in structure but has a thick, toxic atmosphere that traps heat, making it the hottest planet in our solar system.

Source: Wikipedia

Venus has no moons. Its atmosphere is primarily carbon dioxide with clouds of sulfuric acid.
It has a metallic core and rocky mantle similar to Earth’s.
Venus's surface is dry and cracked, with volcanoes and vast plains.
