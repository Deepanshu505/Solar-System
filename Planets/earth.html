EARTH

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to harbor life. About 29.2% of Earth's surface is land with remaining 70.8% is covered with water. Earth's distance from the Sun, physical properties and geological history have allowed life to evolve and thrive.

Source: Wikipedia

Earth is the densest planet in the Solar System and the largest of the terrestrial planets.
Earth's interior consists of a solid inner core, a liquid outer core, a viscous mantle, and a solid crust.
Earth's surface includes mountains, valleys, continents, and ocean basins, shaped by plate tectonics.
