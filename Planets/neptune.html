NEPTUNE

Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun. It’s a cold, dark, and windy ice giant, similar to Uranus. Neptune has the fastest winds in the solar system and 14 known moons, with Triton being the largest.

Source: Wikipedia

Neptune’s atmosphere is made up of hydrogen, helium, and methane, giving it a blue color.
It has a rocky core surrounded by a thick mantle of water, ammonia, and methane ices.
The outer atmosphere has active storms, including the Great Dark Spot.
