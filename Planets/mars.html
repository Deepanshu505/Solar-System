MARS

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is known as the Red Planet due to its reddish appearance caused by iron oxide. It has the tallest volcano and the deepest canyon in the solar system.

Source: Wikipedia

Mars has two small moons, Phobos and Deimos. It’s a target for future colonization.
Its structure includes a core, mantle, and crust similar to Earth.
Mars has vast dust storms, ancient river valleys, and polar ice caps.
