JUPITER

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system and is mostly made of hydrogen and helium. It has a giant storm called the Great Red Spot and more than 75 moons, including Ganymede—the largest moon in the solar system.

Source: Wikipedia

Jupiter has a powerful magnetic field and at least 79 moons.
It likely has a dense core surrounded by a layer of metallic hydrogen.
Jupiter has no solid surface; it’s composed mostly of gas with cloud layers.
