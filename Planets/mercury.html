MERCURY

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and the smallest planet in our solar system. It has a rocky body and a very thin atmosphere, making its surface temperature vary dramatically.

Source: Wikipedia

Mercury has no moons or rings. Its surface is heavily cratered and similar in appearance to the Moon.
The planet has a large metallic core surrounded by a silicate mantle and crust.
The surface of Mercury is marked by impact craters and ancient lava plains.
