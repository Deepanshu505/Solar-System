URANUS

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and has a distinct blue-green color due to methane in its atmosphere. It rotates on its side and has a faint ring system. It’s an ice giant with extreme seasons and cold temperatures.

Source: Wikipedia

Uranus has 27 known moons and a system of narrow, faint rings.
It has a small rocky core surrounded by water, ammonia, and methane ices.
Uranus lacks a solid surface and has a hazy atmosphere of hydrogen, helium, and methane.
