SATURN

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and is famous for its beautiful ring system. It is a gas giant composed mostly of hydrogen and helium. Saturn has more than 80 moons, including Titan, the second-largest moon in the solar system.

Source: Wikipedia

Saturn's rings are made mostly of ice particles with a smaller amount of rocky debris and dust.
It has a small core of rock and metal surrounded by metallic hydrogen.
Saturn has no solid surface; its atmosphere features bands of clouds.
